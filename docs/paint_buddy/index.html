The Game​

Paint Buddy is a mix of of a pixel art editor with a game where you play as Buddy, a charming paint brush that lives to create the most amazing pixel sprites.

You goal is to make beautiful sprites, choose your canvas size and take control of buddy, moving it from side to side, or climbing on the stairs to reach higher spots on the canvas.
